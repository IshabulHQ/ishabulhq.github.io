Projects

Hope Somewhere

Heat map of the United States that provides a unique perspective on population gender balance. Created during HackBU using the Heatmap API to help provide an external view on finding a significant other based on statistics.

Pig Game

Program to play the Pig dice game. Each turn,a player rolls a die until either a 1 is rolled or the player decides to "hold": If the player rolls a 1, they score nothing and it becomes the next player's turn. The first player to score 100 or more points wins.

Digital Clock

Assembled digital clock that displays military time and counts the seconds in binary. A switching regulator was used for USB power and a crowbar circuit was installed for overvoltage protection along with other various components. Upon installation it was programmed in C.
